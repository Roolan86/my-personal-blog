My Story



I was born and raised in the vibrant township of Thabong in the Free State. I'm an introvert who loves meeting new people and doing things out of her comfort zone, yet I love my own company and will always opt to stay indoors and binge my favorite shows (one of them being Catfish on MTV). I studied for 4 years to obtain my Honors degree in Human genetics from the University of the Free State, after graduating I realized my passion lies in Tech, My curiousity and interest in how software works started with my obsession with online shopping (i know, I know, I'm not proud of it but I'm working on it okay.) I tried the whole corporate thing for 9 months and quit to pursue a career in tech. I wanted to change my life and do what I'm passionate about for the rest of my life. I taught myself to code and created my first React app called MovieLand by watching a Youtube tutorial. This was a life-changing moment for me because I fell in love with code and never looked back. Being a self-taught developer was not easy, but I was blessed to have a mentor whom I DM'd on LinkedIn have never met in real-life, and has been so supportive of my journey since. Totally recommend getting a mentor especially when you are transitioning into a whole new industry like myself.
