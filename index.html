My Story


I was born and raised in the vibrant township of Thabong in the Free State. I'm an introvert who loves meeting new people and doing things out of her comfort zone, yet I love my own company and will always opt to stay indoors and binge my favorite shows (one of them being Catfish on MTV). I studied for 4 years to obtain my Honors degree in Human genetics from the University of the Free State, after graduating I realized my passion lies in Tech, My curiousity and interest in how software works started with my obsession with online shopping (I know, I know, I'm not proud of it but I'm working on it okay.) I tried the whole corporate thing for 9 months and quit to pursue a career in tech. I wanted to change my life and do what I'm passionate about for the rest of my life. I taught myself to code and created my first React app called MovieLand by watching a Youtube tutorial. This was a life-changing moment for me because I fell in love with code and never looked back. Being a self-taught developer was not easy, but I was blessed to have a mentor whom I DM'd on LinkedIn but never met in person and has been so supportive of my journey since. Totally recommend getting a mentor especially when you are transitioning into a whole new industry like myself.

From Geneticist to Software developer and Content creator.


Aside from being a software developer, I enjoy creating content on Youtube and Tik Tok. I love shopping online so (you guessed it), I create fashion content on Youtube and share my experiences shopping online especially from international brands. On Tik Tok however I share my experiences and advice on getting into the tech industry without any CS degree and purely bootcamps and certifications. This has been by far the most fulfilling journey to share with my followers because many have also drawn inspiration from me and decided to kickstart their tech careers.
Travelling and trying out new restaurants is my weakness, I've been to Cape Town, Mpumalanga, Durban, Kimberly, Bloem and hope to travel to my dream destination Zanzibar one day. I love that being a software developer will allow me to travel one day.
